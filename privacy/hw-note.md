# Privacy Policy for HwNote

## English

Welcome to HwNote! This application is a Pomodoro timer designed specifically for e-ink screen phones to help you manage your time effectively. Your privacy is critically important to us.

### 1. No Data Collection
HwNote is designed with your privacy as a priority. The application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or personally identifiable data. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, location, device identifiers, or usage patterns.

### 2. Local Data Storage
All data generated by the application, such as your Pomodoro session history, tasks, and settings, is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. This data is not accessible to us or any third party. You have full control over your data, and it is deleted if you uninstall the application.

### 3. No Internet Connection Required
HwNote operates entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not send any data to any servers.

### 4. Permissions
The application requests only the minimum permissions necessary for its core functionality. No permissions are used to track you or collect your data.

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page. Since we do not collect your contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

### 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to open an issue on our project's GitHub page.

# HwNote 隐私政策

## 中文

欢迎使用 HwNote！本应用是一款专为墨水屏手机设计的番茄时间管理软件，旨在帮助您高效地管理时间。我们极其重视您的隐私。

### 1. 不收集任何数据
HwNote 的设计将您的隐私放在首位。本应用不会收集、存储或传输任何个人信息或可识别个人身份的数据。这包括但不限于您的姓名、电子邮件地址、地理位置、设备标识符或使用习惯。

### 2. 数据完全本地存储
应用产生的所有数据（例如您的番茄工作法会话历史、任务和设置）都只存储在您设备的本地存储中。我们或任何第三方都无法访问这些数据。您对自己的数据拥有完全的控制权，当您卸载本应用时，这些数据也会被一并删除。

### 3. 无需网络连接
HwNote 可以完全离线运行。它不需要网络连接即可使用，也不会向任何服务器发送任何数据。

### 4. 权限说明
本应用仅请求其核心功能所必需的最低权限。所有权限都不会用于追踪您或收集您的数据。

### 5. 隐私政策的变更
我们将来可能会更新本隐私政策。任何变更都将在此页面上发布。由于我们不收集您的联系信息，我们建议您定期查看此页面以了解任何更新。

### 6. 联系我们
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，欢迎在我们的项目 GitHub 页面上提交 issue。
